About
N

My five year old niece Maryam asks why until she truly understands. I am exactly the same way.

I am Noer (Misbah) Hameed. People Strategist across five APAC markets. I work on what is hardest to name and easiest to miss: what is actually going on with people, underneath what they show you.

Most HR practice works at the surface level. I work underneath it. I ask the next question, then the next, until the real issue surfaces. Not because I always have a solution. Because every leader deserves an accurate picture, and every person deserves to be genuinely heard.

Focus Areas
01
Manager Disengagement

A disengaged manager costs more than a disengaged employee because the damage travels downward fast. I help organisations identify when this is happening, understand the root cause, and create conditions for recovery before the team pays the price.

02
Quiet Resignation. Except It Is Never Quiet.

By the time someone resigns, the warning signs have been visible for weeks. A drop in initiative. A change in how they speak in meetings. A withdrawal so gradual it looks like professionalism. I help leaders build the diagnostic literacy to catch these signals before a valued person walks out the door.

03
The Cost of Busyness

When workload consistently takes priority over people, the first thing to disappear is psychological safety. Then trust. Then retention. I work with leadership teams to identify where operational pressure is quietly eroding team health and put practical systems in place before the damage compounds.

04
Connection, Validation, Purpose

These three things sit beneath every retention problem, every disengagement conversation, and every team that stops performing. I help leaders understand where these fundamentals are breaking down in their specific context and build manager capability to restore them systematically.
